In the Great Continent of Eurasia, after the great swordsman Gurianos downed the devilish warrior Gildus, peace returned to Eurasia and its people completely forgotten the darkness sealed by the evil spirit
Five years later, while living in the interior of Eurasia, Guarianos learns from a passing fencer that Gildus has been resurrected
Gurianos once again hits the road to the Golden Castle to find out the truth.

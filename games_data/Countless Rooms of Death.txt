Countless Rooms of Death is a puzzle / adventure game with a horror atmosphere
There are no weapons or real enemies; instead, we aim to bully your mind
All you, the player, have is a flashlight with which to find your way through a dark labyrinth, solving puzzles to acquire keys which will allow you to enter the next level

 
There are strange happenings and whispered voices to investigate, and several ways to meet your demise within the labyrinth
Strange lights and shapes will try to distract you from your path to freedom
The story unfolds in the form of medical records which, with the passage of time, will help you to remember how you got here in the first place.

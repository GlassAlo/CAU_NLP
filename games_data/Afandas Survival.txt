Afandas Survival is an open world survival game
After a plague wipes out most of the population, you're one of the few survivors on an island called Afanda

Already at the beginning you realize that surviving isn´t easy
To stay alive, you must search for equipment and provisions
To make life easier watch out for recipes to enhance your crafting skills

Hunger isn’t the only danger awaiting you on Afandas
Danger is all around you
Be it in form of wild animals, walking dead or other players where you can never know is it a friend or a foe
Are you goanna make it?

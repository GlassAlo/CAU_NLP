It’s your time to be the hero and help cute little dino to safely reach its destination
There will be obstacles on the way and time constraints; you have to beat the clock and your enemies to make your way to the end.

Little dino wants your help as there are hurdles in his way, enemies, snow balls, bombs, laser and fire
It is up to you to use your skills and take him through the checkpoints
If you get hit by an obstacle or enemy, you will lose a life
There are different levels in the game
Each level has a different location and set up, which gives an enhanced gameplay experience.

Salient Features
• Amazing Graphics
• Exceptional Gameplay
• Multiplayer Option (Beta)
• Dynamic effects and sound quality
• Easy control

The excitement of this game doubles up in the Battle Arena
It allows multiple players to play together and enjoy the game
In the Battle Area, players get to play in a Knockback combat
Each player has to hit their competitors and knock them out of the area
They can use two types of fires to win the battle by knocking their enemies out.

The player who will get maximum hits will be knocked out of the arena
Thus, it will be the survival of fittest
You can play this game with your friends and show your gaming abilities.

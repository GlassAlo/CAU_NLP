Love Hina Party puts you in shoes of Keitaro himself as he resumes his duties of a hotel manager
During adventure mode, you can check certain predefined locations and speak with characters you meet, sometimes selecting your answer during conversation
Sometimes during the story, the girls in Hinata apartments will ask you quiz questions about the Love Hina anime
The action mode in the game is done via four different mini-games which, unlike the adventure mode, use 3rd-person perspective.

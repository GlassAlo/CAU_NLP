Battle Mages is a courageous mix of the RTS and RPG in a huge fantasy world, full of mystery, mythical creatures, magic and knights honor.
Imagine as you have just graduated of an Imperial Magic School and now you have to face the trial of Battle Mages Guild completing the tasks in the Mage Towers all around the Internal World
Start your glorious adventure by fighting through the undead army to find the lost gnome artifact that can stop the imminent danger.
Your time is running out my friend! Challenge yourself and stop this bloody war! Let the magic be with you!

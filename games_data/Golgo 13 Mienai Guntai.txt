Golgo 13 - Mienai Guntai (Also known as Golgo 13 Kenenai Guntai) is the second Playstation one game based on the manga series written and illustrated by Takao Saito, published in Shogakukan's Big Comic magazine since 1969
In 1976, the manga won the 21st Shogakukan Manga Award for general manga
The series follows the titular character, a professional assassin for hire.

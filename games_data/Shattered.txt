Shattered is a first-person game made by developer Devin Eastmond
Inspired by Mark Hadley's Slender: The Eight Pages, it explores genres of horror, fantasy, action, and survival
It follows the experiences of a brave villager who attempts to save his homeland from the threat of nocturnal zombies
When confronted by this menace, will you have courage and continue with the mission, or will you abandon all hope in an attempt to save your own life?

Kohan: Ahriman’s Gift is a real-time strategy game set in the fantasy world of Khaldun
Built on the a Kohan: Immortal Sovereigns gameplay, Ahriman’s Gift is the stand-alone prequel to Kohan: Immortal Sovereigns
Experience the world of Khaldun from a whole new perspective: evil
Lead armies of Shadowbeasts and Undead as you ravage the countryside, driving all enemies before you
Ahriman’s Gift not only provides new campaigns and improved artificial intelligence, but also introduces dozens of new heroes and units to control.

Heroes Chronicles: The Fiery Moon is the sixth episode of the Heroes Chronicles series
Alongside Heroes Chronicles: The World Tree, it was released as a bonus in downloadable format on 3DO's official website between the release of the first four Chronicles and The Final Chapters

The campaign is a direct sequel, concluding the story arc commenced in The World Tree
As Vorr flees Enroth for a deserted, post-apocalyptic world, Tarnum and his Stronghold armies pursue, seeking to free the two remaining Ancestors from their imprisonment and destroy their insane counterpart.

A retro inspired, platforming RPG
Set in a young boys world of dreams and nightmares
Level up, collect masks, unlock unique abilities, choose stats, and take on challenging bosses
Using fast action, skill shots, and responsive player controls, Asher allows for a challenging and enjoyable game play experience
While playing as Asher it is up to you to take on the things haunting your dreams, find your sister Lila, and bring her back home.

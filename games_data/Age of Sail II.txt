Age of Sail II simulates naval warfare from 1775 to 1820 with a real-time 3-D sailing and combat model
Over 1,200 historical ships and 100 historical American and British scenarios are available, including: Copenhagen, the Battle of the Nile, Trafalgar, Cape St
Vincent, and Camperdown
A full, floating 3-D camera allows complete control of your fleet, and the 3-D accelerated graphics reveal accurately rendered ships and beautiful landscapes

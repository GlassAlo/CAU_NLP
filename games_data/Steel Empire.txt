Steel Empire is a steam punk shoot'em up game featuring a frantic combat filled with powerful enemies and insane obstacles
Enjoy a beautiful world and retro game-play as you dodge and shoot your way through classical stages
Choose one of 4 difficulty settings according to your set of skills and fight the evil empire! See how well you did using the Replay Mode and show your worth by getting all achievements.

Originally released on the Sega Genesis / Mega Drive consoles in 1992, it was later ported to the Nintendo Game Boy Advance in 2004 and to the Nintendo 3DS in 2014, and now is coming to PC!

Fullmetal Alchemist and the Broken Angel, is an action role-playing game developed by Racjin and published by Square Enix for the PlayStation 2 console and is the first ever Fullmetal Alchemist video game released
The game features an original story by Hiromu Arakawa
The game was released in Japan on December 25th 2003 and in North America on January 18, 2005.

Adrenalin is a arcade racer/management game published by 1C games.

Adrenalin is a game that successfully blends the genres of thrilling adrenalin-pumping racing and an economic management sim
The aim of a player is to not only become the fastest driver around, but to be a talented manager as well
In the beginning of the game the player has to pick out one of the twelve beautiful young ladies
One of them even exists in reality - she is the winner of Miss Russia contest in 2003.

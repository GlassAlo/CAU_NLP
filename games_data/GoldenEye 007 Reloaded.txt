The GoldenEye story comes to life once again with an updated single player storyline featuring Daniel Craig as Bond and written by Bruce Feirstein, the screenwriter for the original movie.

Surprise enemies covertly or engage in a full on firefight and use Bond's latest gadget to uncover intel in augmented reality as you relive all the classic GoldenEye movie moments
Infiltrate the dam and destroy the facility; chase Ourumov in the tank through the streets of St
Petersburgh; and investigate the secret jungle base.

The remastered game features high-definition graphics due to running on a new game engine, an achievement system, a new Mi6 Ops mission mode, and PlayStation Move compatibility for the PS3 version.

Activision also released the GoldenEye 007: Reloaded - Double 'O' Edition which included access to the downloadable character Hugo Drax, a PlayStation Move motion controller, navigation controller, PlayStation Eye camera, and Sharp Shooter peripheral along with the game.

Fast thinking and reflexes are required in order for you to survive all the traps and the attacks of your enemies
It will try to surprise you on your every move, and most probably succeed
And when you think you have finally won and you are safe, something will go wrong and make your little world a living hell
This game is specially made for people who want some real challenge, so don't get disappointed if you die, even on the first few levels
Be quick and resourceful and enjoy yourself.

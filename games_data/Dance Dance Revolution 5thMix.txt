Dance Dance Revolution 5thMIX, or DDR 5th Mix, is the 5th game in the Dance Dance Revolution series of music video games
It was released to the arcades by Konami on March 27, 2001
Although only officially released in Japan, units exist worldwide
DDR 5th Mix contains a total of 122 songs, nine of which are hidden and unlockable
Of those songs, 40 of them (including all nine unlockable songs) are brand new to Dance Dance Revolution.

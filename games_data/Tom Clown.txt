Tom Clown is an unlicensed game for the Sega Mega Drive developed by AV artisan and published by Realtec in 1993.

Tom Clown Circus is an interpretation of the game with the Atari 2600
The game is pretty hardcore
Music is poor
A "diversity" levels - a typical Arkanoid without bonuses
It was originally bundled on floppy with Realtec's copier and was eventually put out on an extremely small amount cartridges.

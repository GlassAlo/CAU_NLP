Muri is a DOS-style shooter from Remar Games and Ludosity
After the colonization of Mars, a team of scientists design an armor suit of cataclysmic power, sparking a conflict between several factions
When Mars suddenly vanishes, one woman sets out to discover the fate of humanity.

Inspired by the games of its time, Muri is an authentic '80s experience, with the focus often changing between run-and-gun action and more tactical combat.

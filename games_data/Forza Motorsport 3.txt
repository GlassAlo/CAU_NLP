Forza Motorsport 3 is a racing video game developed for Xbox 360 by Turn 10 Studios
It was released in October 2009
It is the sequel to Forza Motorsport 2 and the third installment in the Forza Motorsport series
The game includes more than 400 customizable cars (More than 500 cars in the Ultimate Collection version) from 50 manufacturers and more than 100 race track variations with the ability to race up to eight cars on track at a time
These cars vary from production cars to race cars such as those from the American Le Mans Series.[1]

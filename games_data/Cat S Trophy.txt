Cat 'S' Trophy is a KART management game
Race with one of five chosen cat racers summoned by the cat god (ruler of the internets, pioneer of new realms, and giver of burgers) to race for the cat 'S' trophy
You, human helper, have been brought here to direct the games, guide the cats, and aid in your cat partners glory by sabotaging the other contestants to collect the most star stuff and win the race!

Kid Dracula is a platformer video game made by Konami
It was released only in Japan for the Famicom on 19 October 1990
It is considered a parody of the Castlevania series
A version for mobile phones was also released on 30 June 2006 in Japan only.

The game saw ports for the PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, and Microsoft Windows as part of the Castlevania Anniversary Collection which was released digitally on May 16, 2019 in all regions.

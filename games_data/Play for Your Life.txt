The player controls a robot with a bat attempting to hit a ball at a circular target on the opposing wall
Alternatively the player may try to kill his opponent by hitting him with the ball or bat
The computer-controlled opposing player is trying to do the same.

As the game progresses more balls are brought into play, up to a limit of four
A round is won when one player scores three goals or kills his opponent.

There are 26 levels of varying difficulty
Later levels feature movable barriers and various other obstacles to complicate play.

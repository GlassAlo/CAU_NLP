Chaos Breaker/Dark Awake: The King Has No Name is a fantasy-themed sprite-based 2D fighting game developed by Eolith and released exclusively in Japan arcades
Players select a team of 3 characters, similar to The King of Fighters series
There are 18 playable characters, 3 bosses, and six different races featured in the game, including: Human, Dark Elf, Elf, Orc, Dwarf, and Undead.

Milestone Shooting Collection 2 has everything a shooter wants
It contains: Radirgy, Radirgy Noa, Chaos Field, Karous and Ilvero, almost all of the remarkable titles the studio has created
Each of them feature a unique world full of bullets and captivating systems
Ilbero takes place in a net game sort of world and your shooting craft takes care of attack, defense and the bash-on action
Radirgy takes place in a hip electronic world and Radirgy Noa has a double player mode, so ask a friend to join your game
Chaos Field pits you against bosses the moment you enter the game, but Karous gives you a smoother learning curve, so beginners can start here before heading off to the more challenging games.

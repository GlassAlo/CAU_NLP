Choose from 24 animated chess sets, including Mycenae, Egyptian, Isle of Lewis, and Stanton in Chessmaster II, a chess strategy board game
Try to checkmate your friends, or challenge one of the 64 computer opponents (each with a different style of play and varying degrees of skill), or try to beat one of the 30 Grandmaster profiles
You'll never miss an opening in the 3D mode, since you can rotate the camera to any angle you want
Or, if you prefer, play on a flat 2D board
Chessmaster II brings the thrills and challenges of chess to life.

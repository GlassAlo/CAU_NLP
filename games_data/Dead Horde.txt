As a survivor of the mutant virus together with a comrade or alone, you must make your way through the city levels, every new area uncovering new challenges to overcome and mutants to destroy
With a large variety of weapons at your disposal, together you must wipe out the hordes of flesh eating mutants
Teamwork is the key to survive the DEAD HORDE...Key features:Dead Horde is an action packed, co-op top down, survival-horror shooter

 
Play the campaign in single-player, local co-op or online co-op with a friend
Make your way through the levels with a huge variety of weapons
Use assault rifles, shotguns, grenades, mini-guns and a lot more to unlock during the game.

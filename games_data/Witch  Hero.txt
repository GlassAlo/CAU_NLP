Witch & Hero is an 8-bit style action game
You play as a hero accompanied by a witch taking on the evil Medusa, whom has destroyed many villages and towns with the monsters that stand before her.

Medusa turned the witch into stone, so she is no longer able to move
So it's up to the hero to defeat the monsters and collect their blood in order to recover the witch for a limited duration
The witch's destructive magic easily destroys monsters, but you will have to rely on the hero's will power primarily
He is able to try and try again, but the witch cannot, thus the hero must protect her.

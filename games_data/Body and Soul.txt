“Body&Soul” is a game of logic and skill, with interesting and fun mechanics.

Use your robot and show him what to do in order to solve puzzles in an unordinary spirit world
Beat various game stages using your mind, skill and speed
Beat in game bosses
Discover new ways to complete puzzles to achieve a 3 star rating and much more!

Terrorist Takedown: War in Colombia is a videogame for the PC, developed by the Polish company City Interactive in 2006.
Your name is Jack Jeffers, and your mission is to fight alongside allied Colombian and American forces, while still receiving orders from time to time and obeying special orders of your own
Penetrate the drug lords' massive defenses and turn the tide of the jungle war in this first-person jungle shooter
War in Colombia is the fourth game in the series.

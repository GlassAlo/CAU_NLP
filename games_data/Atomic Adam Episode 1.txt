Atomic Adam: Episode 1 is a gaming experience packed to the brim with nostalgic retro action! Fight, fly and shoot your way through a fully 3D world filled with giant rats, grey aliens, UFO's, boss battles and insane combat, all with an art style inspired by the retro action games of yesterday
As rock star Atomic Adam, you'll battle your way through Delta City defeating bosses on your hunt for the infamous Deathbot
None is more mysterious than Deathbot who traveled to Delta City with his army to usurp the city and its resources
Atomic Adam is tasked with finding Mayor Lamowitz's wife who was kidnapped by Deathbot and now held hostage
Deathbot now demands Mayor Lamowitz to resign or he will never see his wife again! 
 
Do you have what it takes to save Delta City before it falls in to the hands of Deathbot? Atomic Adam represents a new story in the Radical Heroes Universe
Complete with a new hero, game play mechanics, story and action all in the retro inspired universe of Radical Heroes.

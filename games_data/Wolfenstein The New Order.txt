Wolfenstein: The New Order is an action-adventure shooter game played from a first-person perspective
It is the semi-sequel to 2009's Wolfenstein, however utilizes very little from the game and drops any direct references to veil or supernatural.

To progress through the story, the player battle enemies throughout levels
The game utilizes a health system in which players' health is divided into separate sections that regenerate; if an entire section is lost, the player must use a health pack to replenish the missing health
In combat, a cover system can be used as assistance against enemies
The player have the ability to lean around, over, and under cover, which can be used as a tactical advantage during shootouts and stealth levels

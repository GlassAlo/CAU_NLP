Dezaemon 3D is a video game and game creator for the Nintendo 64
It was released only in Japan in 1998
The game's editor allows players to design their own shooting game levels similar to the levels shown in Star Soldier: Vanishing Earth
The game has a large number of options, from creating the stage boss or adding a custom soundtrack for each level
It was originally developed alongside an ultimately unreleased accompanying expansion disk title for 64DD.
